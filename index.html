Biography

I am a Ph.D student at School of Life Science and Technology, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), Chengdu, Sichuan, China, and working with Dr. Tao Zhang and Dr. Xueming Zou in the Key Laboratory for Neuro Information of Ministry of Education. Before that I recieved my master degree and bachelor degree in the School of Computer Science and Technology, Southwest University of Science and Technology (SWUST), Mianyang, Sichuan, China in Jun. 2016 and Jun. 2013, respectively.

My research interest mainly focuses on digital image & video processing, machine learning & deep learning. Recently, I have been working on the low-level computer vision tasks of medical images with deep learning techniques, including image restoration and reconstruction, super-resolution, and recognition.
