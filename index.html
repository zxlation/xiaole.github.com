Biography

I worked with Dr. Tao Zhang and Dr. Xueming Zou in the Key Laboratory for NeuroInformation of Ministry of Education, and received my Ph.D degree at School of Life Science and Technology, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), Chengdu, China. I am now an assistant professor in the school of information science and technology of Southwest Jiaotong University (SWJTU).

My research interest mainly focuses on digital image & video processing, machine learning & deep learning. Recently, I have been working on the low-level computer vision tasks of medical images with deep learning techniques, including image restoration and reconstruction, super-resolution, and recognition.
